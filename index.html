About Me

I've always been involved in the arts. I often work in watercolor, and most recently, digitally using Procreate on an iPad. I double-majored in studio art and Russian language in college, have sold commissioned art, and in December 2022, I had my first booth at an art fair. I have taught several subjects to students from kindergarten to university level. I taught K-12 art, middle school English and Social Studies, and high school World History at an international school in Prague. After that I went to graduate school and earned a PhD in art history, and taught as an art history professor for seven years. I decided to take a class on programming because I wanted to be able to live closer to my family. Several people suggested looking into programming because of its creative aspects.
